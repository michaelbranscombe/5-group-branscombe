Technology.

Making the world more, NOT less, social.

Introduction

The use of technology has been steadily increasing in our lives, and with that, the use of social media and other online interaction services have never been more in demand. Some critics of this technology have stated that it makes us less social, or that the interaction we do have online is not as enriching as a "real-life" interaction with another person. In reality, however, the rapid advancement of technology has made us more social, rather than less. Using instant messaging (SMS, or text), Snapchat, Instagram, Facebook, Xbox Live, or any other social networking service helps us communicate and interact with people that are not always reachable otherwise, due to travel, or any other circumstance. Therefore, technology has helped society reach new social heights and has extended the range at which people can be reached in.
